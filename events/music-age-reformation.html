Music in the Age of Reformation

BBC Proms star vocal ensemble, I Fagiolini, perform a unique, specially selected arrangement exploring the impact of the Reformation on music.

Explore the Reformation with I Fagiolini

Acclaimed vocal ensemble I Fagiolini will perform a specially selected programme exploring the impact of the Reformation on English music. This is a unique chance to hear this exciting group in concert in an intimate venue before their appearance at the 2017 BBC Proms.

This arrangement has been specially curated by I Fagiolini as a bespoke event for Senate House Library and will be available on this night only. The performance will include a brief interval and includes some complimentary refreshment.

About I Fagiolini

I Fagiolini - Monteverdi's 'Laudate Pueri' from their 450th Monteverdi anniversary celebration.

Grounded in the classics of Renaissance and Twentieth-century vocal repertoire, I Fagiolini is renowned for its innovative and often staged productions of this music. It has staged Handel with masks, Purcell with puppets, and in 2004 premiered The Full Monteverdi, a ground-breaking dramatised account of the composer’s Fourth Book of Madrigals (1603) by John La Bouchardière, since turned into a highly successful film shown all over the world. In 2006 I Fagiolini toured its South African collaboration Simunye and in 2009 created Tallis in Wonderland, a new way of hearing polyphony with live and recorded voices.

I Fagiolini are currently working on a year long celebration of Monteverdi's 450th anniversary, Living Monteverdi and have sold out their perfromance for this year's BBC proms in under 2 hours.
