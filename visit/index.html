Visit

Reformation: Shattered world, new beginnings was an exhibition at Senate House Library that ran between June and December 2017. Although the physical exhibition is no longer open, our online legacy platform still allows you to view the exhibition galleries, and check our extensive collection of Reformation resources and blog posts.

The Library runs exhibitions and events throughout the year. See our Exhibitions and Events pages for details.
